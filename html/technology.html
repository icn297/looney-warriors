03 WELCOME TO THE CREW OF GOODNESS!

The rumours... Loaylty

Loyalty (aka: Be more reliable than a cartoon character in a chase scene!) Loyalty is key, folks! Think of it like Wile E. Coyote chasing the Road Runner. Never gives up, always comes back (sometimes with a new plan... sometimes with a very big hammer). If you’re committed to this mission, you're in the right place. But remember, loyalty doesn’t mean you have to wear a cape—just be the friend who shows up when you say you will. 🦸‍♂️

The rumours... No hierachy

2. No Hierarchy (We’re all just running in the same direction, no giant anvils here) Forget about those silly power structures! We believe in equality—no leader, no followers, just a bunch of crazy, fun-loving individuals with one goal: protect the goodness. It’s like Bugs Bunny leading the charge... one carrot at a time. No bossing around, just fun-loving cooperation and a dash of slapstick chaos. 😜

The rumours... Love

3. Love (It’s the kind of love that even Daffy Duck would agree on) Love isn’t just a feeling, it’s our main fuel. We’re talking about love for each other, love for the planet, and the kind of love that says, “I’ll share my last slice of pizza with you even if you’re being a bit... extra.” 💖 If you’re down for this kind of love-fueled mission, you’re already part of the crew (even if you’re a little wacko).

Rules to Keep the Fun (and Safety) Alive: No drugs or nicotine. Seriously, folks. The only thing you should be inhaling is good vibes and maybe some coffee. (Okay, a little bit of coffee. Not too much! We don’t want you bouncing off the walls like Taz on a sugar high. ☕) No drugs of any kind. Except for... you guessed it... coffee! Because sometimes, the mission requires that extra burst of energy. (But let’s be real, even Bugs Bunny needs his "carrot juice" in the morning!)

Think About This... We’re already sitting in a biological spaceship (a.k.a. Earth), zooming around in space like we’re in one big, weird cartoon. 🌌 What if all this crazy stuff we’re doing is part of a much bigger cartoon universe? What if we’re the stars of a show—like, the universe’s greatest comedy sketch? Think about it... or don’t. We’re not here to ruin your morning, but it’s fun to imagine, right? 🚀🎬

OUR MISSION: To protect the goodness, spread joy, and keep the world spinning on the right axis (even if it’s a bit wobbly sometimes). We're like a team of lovable misfits, but we get the job done—whether it’s saving the day or just sharing a good laugh. 🌟 Ready to join the crew? Then step up, get wacky, and remember... if we can do it, you sure can! There you go, with all the humor and fun mixed in, now you're ready to recruit your crew with a smile and a wink! 😁
